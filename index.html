SOUTHERN HOSPITALITY

Fishing in the Gulf, Jul 2, 2015

PE+

There was nothing more relaxing than feeling the cool breeze, during the mid summer on the gulf. I visit my brother every summer in Mobile, Alabama. There is not much to do there, so fishing became a hobby. Similarly to Tennessee, which is where I was originally born, Mobile, Alabama has great outdoor activitites. That is if you're into hunting, fishing, and nature. I was borned and raised in Memphis, Tennessee. I have been driving trucks for a living since 2017. Although, truck driving is a cool career, I never intended it to be doing it for the rest of my life. I was always into tech, but I never thought about pursuing it because when I was younger, I was not ready to accept it's challenges. I knew it would be very difficult to learn, so I decided to try different career paths in college. I didn't finish with a degree because I was undecisive about choosing a major. Truck driving allowed me to make good money, but I always felt that I could do more. As I became older, I became more willing to take on more of a difficult task in tech. Only, I didn't think I would be able to obtain a career in tech without a college degree. Throughout the years, I have been hearing more and more about bootcamps online that can lead you to careers like software engineer. That's why I am here today. I want to take on the challenge, change my career, and become a software engineer. It would be a proud accomplishment.
